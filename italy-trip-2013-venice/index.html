Italy Trip 2012: Venice

When I was planning this trip to Lake Garda, it occurred to me that I could possibly squeeze in a couple of days visit to Venice and also Verona on the way back, I have been to both these places before but never with a decent camera! and as before, the weather in Venice was up and down (a week later it was flooded! a lucky escape indeed..), but you don’t really worry about the weather when in such a magnificent city on water! just getting the train across the long bridge over the lagoon and being greeted by water buses is enough to get the adventure fest going..
