Barcelona 2013: Tibidabo & City

Ahh.. Barcelona! well it’s every bit as good as your friends & guidebooks tell you! being my first time there I booked up a whole week to explore the place and that what I did. Before heading off on my travels I saw a photo of the Temple del Sagrat Cor, it kinda reminded me of the Christ the Redeemer statue in Brazil and I loved the angle of the shot the photographer had taken ~ I wanted this photograph! and I’m glad to say I got it (first photo of this set) ~ getting all that church into one photo requires going up in an unusual bit of machinery! I do have a picture but I’m gonna leave it to the imagination, just like when I first saw the pic back home..
