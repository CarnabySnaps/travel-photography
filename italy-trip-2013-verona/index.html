Italy Trip 2012: Verona

'O Romeo, Romeo! wherefore art thou Romeo’ I’ve been to Verona before and that was only due to a past girlfriends love of Shakespeare, this place ended up on our travel itinerary back then and I’m glad it did ~ what a great city! it kinda reminds me of a Italian version of London, lot’s going on, great architecture but minus the gloomy bits ~ the Arena is still has the romanesque quality about it, Lamberti Tower has some great views of the city & Juliet’s balcony is still adorned by love struck teenagers (and adults alike).
